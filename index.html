Click on the cells in the grid to activate/deactivate them and create a pattern of beats. You can also use the mouse to click and drag to activate/deactivate multiple cells at once. The "play" button will start the sequencer, while the "pause" button will pause it. You can also use the space bar on your keyboard to pause the sequencer. To use the piano roll, press and hold down certain keys on your keyboard. Use the displayed piano keys as a guide to know which keys to play on the keyboard. Have fun experimenting with different patterns of beats and adjusting the tempo and volume to create your own unique sound! Press "Fableton" to start your musical journey!
