Open the web page containing the sequencer in a web browser. Click on the cells in the grid to activate/deactivate them and create a pattern of beats. You can also use the mouse to click and drag to activate/deactivate multiple cells at once. Use the buttons at the top of the page to control the sequencer. The "play" button will start the sequencer, while the "pause" button will pause it. You can also use the spacebar on your keyboard to pause the sequencer. The "BPM" will adjust the tempo of the sequencer. THe slider labeled "Volume" to adjust the overall volume of the sequencer. Use the "Mute" button to mute/unmute the sequencer. Use the rows of cells labeled "Kick," "Snare," "Hi-Hat," and "Bass" to create different types of beats. Click on the cells to activate/deactivate them and create a pattern of beats. To use the piano roll, press and hold down certain keys on your keyboard. Use the displayed piano keys as a guide to know which keys to play on the keyboard. Have fun experimenting with different patterns of beats and adjusting the tempo and volume to create your own unique music! Press "Fabelton" to start your musical journey!
